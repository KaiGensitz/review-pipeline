NPV (precision of AI exclusions): 100.0% (95% CI 100.0-100.0%)

Interpretation (plain language):
- Sensitivity: fraction of human-relevant papers the AI kept.
- Specificity: fraction of human-irrelevant papers the AI excluded.
- PPV/NPV: how often AI include/exclude calls match human labels.
- PABAK adjusts kappa for class imbalance, stabilizing agreement estimates.
